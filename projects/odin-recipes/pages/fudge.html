Fudge

Ingredients

  • 1 stick Fastco oleo (not butter)
  • 1 (13 oz) can evaporated milk
  • 3 to 4 cups sugar
  • 1 bag chocolate chips
  • 1 jar marshmallow creme

Instructions

  1. Bring oleo, milk, and sugar to a boil until it doesnâ€™t stir down anymore.
  2. Boil for 9 minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Remove from stove.
  4. Add chocolate chips and marshmallow creme.
  5. Mix it well.
  6. Put into a buttered 9x13 inch pan.
  7. Cool at room temp until firm.
